Documenting Gujarat

This is a photographic site documenting rural lives in Gujarat, India. The pictures were all taken by me, during an educational trip to this Western Indian state.

The purpose of the trip was to conduct an ethncographic study on the locals of Gujarat. These locals live in their own bubble and have formed a colorful, beautiful world of their own. The study trip lasted for 4 days, with multiple documenting methods like journaling, photographing, and recording audio interviews.
